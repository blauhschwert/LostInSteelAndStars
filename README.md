# 🚀 Lost in Steel and Stars

A dungeon crawler about a clumsy yet curious hero trapped inside a mysterious futuristic ship drifting through space.

## 🎮 About the Game

You awaken in a massive, cold steel labyrinth — a futuristic dungeon ship far from Earth.  
With no memory of how you got there, you must explore, survive, and uncover the truth.

You are not a perfect hero.  
You stumble. You make mistakes.  
But your curiosity drives you forward.

Each room reveals new dangers, puzzles, and fragments of a forgotten story.  
The deeper you go, the closer you get to understanding how to escape… and return home.

## 🧠 Features

- 🧩 Puzzle-based dungeon exploration  
- ⚙️ Futuristic sci-fi setting  
- 🎭 Story told through environment and discovery  
- 💀 Trial-and-error progression (learn from failure)  
- 🔁 Replayable exploration with hidden secrets  

Dungeon crawler games often focus on exploration, procedural spaces, and survival mechanics :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0} — this project builds on those ideas with a narrative-driven twist.

## 🕹️ Gameplay

- Explore interconnected rooms inside a massive ship  
- Solve puzzles to unlock new areas  
- Avoid traps and unknown systems  
- Discover logs and clues about the ship  
- Find a way back to Earth
